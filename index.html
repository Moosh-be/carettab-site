New Tab page replacement for Chrome, Firefox, and Edge

A beautifully simplistic, fully customizable, New Tab page with multiple clocks, date, weather, search, bookmarks, custom backgrounds, and more!

Features

  • Display time and date on new tab page.
  • Digital/Analog/Binary clock options.
  • Add additional clocks with labels.
  • Customizable timezones for all clocks.
  • Display current weather and forecast.
  • Search Yahoo, Google, Bing, Baidu and more from new tab page.
  • Display favorite links for quick access and the bookmarks bar.
  • Include a custom message on the page.
  • Choose from several different color themes or choose your own custom colors.
  • Use various provided patterns or your own background image.
  • Select your desired size, spacing, and placement of everything.
  • Choose from a handful of fonts or use any installed on your machine.
  • Customize everything! Toggle the time, seconds, time format, date, date format, search engine, 24 hour time, week numbers, tab title and more.
  • Sync support. Keep your settings across all devices. (Requires storage permissions)
  • Multiple languages supported!
  • Available on Chrome, Firefox, and Edge!

A beautifully simplistic New Tab page that includes multiple clocks, date, weather, search, quick links, and more! Fully customizable! If you are looking for a complete personalized experience, then CaretTab is the New Tab page for you! Everything can be changed from multiple clocks with different time zones, to Quick Links, to the spacing between numbers within the time! Have colleagues in other time zones? Add as many additional clocks as will fit on your screen! Don't like remembering web addresses? Display both a bookmarks bar and custom quick links, as many as you like! Researching a topic? Use the search bar to search Yahoo or all kinds of other search engines. Don't have windows? Display the weather on your new tab. You can also change the look all you want with background and foreground colors, background patterns, or a custom background image. Everything can be adjusted to the size you want, and can be rearranged how you like. Download now and make it yours!

Make the New Tab page exactly what you want. From minimalist to a statement piece, the choice is yours! The goal of CaretTab is full customization allowing you to make it as simple or complex as you like. With the CaretTab New Tab page you get complete choice in how your start page looks.

The main focus of this new tab page is the clock. You have all the options at your disposal here. You choose the time zone you use. Set 12 or 24 hour time. Optionally display seconds, meridiem (am/pm), delimiters, labels and more. You can even choose an analog or binary clock design. Need to track time in multiple locations? Add as many additional clocks, all of which are just as customizable.

Display the date next to the clock. You can choose which time zone that date is based on. Display it in any format you wish! Optionally display the year, month, day, and day of week. A week number option is also available.

Make it fit your personality with color and background image options! You can choose from a preset list or custom colors for both the background and foreground. Select from one of the many built-in patterns or choose your own photo as the new tab background! Set filters on the background for a customized look, ensuring the text remains clear. Select from many fonts or your own font installed on your machine!

Enable weather to see current conditions and forecast. Optionally enable current weather or the forecast or both! Make it yours by choosing fahrenheit or celsius, metric or imperial, toggle the degree symbol, toggle weather icons, set number of days in forecast, and more!

Get quick access to your favorite websites via two distinct options. Display the bookmarks bar to show a list of your favorites saved in the browser. Or choose the Quick Links option to manually add any link you like. Favicons can be optionally displayed as well as browser links such as bookmark manager, history, and most visited.

Search the web from the new tab page. Choose from a wide range of search engines from Yahoo to GitHub. Enter your own label and choose the size of the search bar. Add a custom greeting of your choice to the new tab with complete options for the size it displays. Even the tab's title can be changed to a custom message, the time, date, and more! Available in multiple languages!
